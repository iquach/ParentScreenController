No longer will children be allowed unlimited access to apps when they should be studying!

Parents will be able to create an account on the device and set periods of time when apps can be used.

Once this time is reached apps that have been specified will be closed and they will not be allowed to open until the time period is over.

A user can also choose to start restricting apps immediately without setting a time on the main menu.
